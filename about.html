About tiny.lol

Tiny.lol is a URL shortening service that provides all the important URL shortening features you need for a fraction of the price compared to competitors.

We provide the following features:

  1. Free short links without registration
  2. Unlimited link clicks
  3. Link management and analytics1
  4. Unlimited active links
  5. Vanity links1&2
  6. Analytics history, timechart, map, time of day and devices1
  7. API and bulk shortening1,2&3
  8. Link-in-bio pages1&3
  9. Link-in-bio custom themes
  10. Link-in-bio forms
  11. Link groups
  12. Free & unlimited 2D code generator

1: Features requires a free registration

2: Certain limits may be applied for free users

3: Certain limits may be applied for extended usage

Story of the app's development

Tiny.lol was started in February 2024 by Stanislav Vasilev. At the time, the paywalls for services like bit.ly and linktree seemed unreasonable to him, so he decided to start a new service that would combine the features of the 2 platforms and provide more free features while making the premium tiers cheaper. The idea that kickstarted work on the project was a cheap auction for the tiny.lol domain on Namecheap.
